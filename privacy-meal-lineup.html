Meal Lineup

Privacy Policy

Kiki Gadgets Ltd. · Effective Date: May 9, 2026

Kiki Gadgets Ltd. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Meal Lineup mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile photo (if provided via Google or Apple Sign-In).
  • Family profile: household size and dietary preferences you configure within the App.
  • Meal plans and custom recipes you create, including ingredients and cooking instructions.
  • Community recipes you choose to submit for publication.
  • Images you upload to accompany your recipes, stored in Firebase Storage.

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: features accessed, meal planning interactions, and shopping list activity.
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Authentication tokens for connected third-party services (e.g., Kroger) stored securely on your device using encrypted storage.
  • Crash reports, performance diagnostics, and aggregate product-usage analytics via Firebase Analytics.

c) Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: name, email address, and profile photo from your connected account.
  • Kroger API: store location data and cart interactions when you choose to connect a Kroger-family store account.
  • Walmart Affiliate Product API: when you choose to send your shopping list to Walmart, the names of items on your list are sent through our secure Firebase Cloud Function to Walmart's product search API to find matching products. We do not transmit your account, email, location, or other personal information to Walmart in this step. After matching, we open Walmart's website or app on your device with the items pre-loaded into a cart; once you reach Walmart, your interaction with their service is governed by Walmart's own privacy policy.
  • Spoonacular: recipe-search queries you enter in the Discover tab (e.g., cuisine, meal type, ingredients, dietary filters) are sent to Spoonacular to retrieve matching recipes.
  • Google Gemini AI: ingredient lists from your meal plans, individual ingredient text, and recipe URLs you import are sent to Google's Gemini API for: (a) parsing imported recipes into structured ingredients and instructions; (b) normalizing and consolidating similar ingredients across recipes (for example, combining "1 lb ground beef" from one recipe with "2 lb ground beef" from another into a single shopping-list entry). Inputs are not used to train Google's models per the Gemini API Terms.
  • RevenueCat: subscription status and purchase history for premium features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Meal Lineup App and its features.
  • Personalise meal recommendations based on your dietary preferences and family size.
  • Generate shopping lists and facilitate grocery ordering through connected stores.
  • Display community recipes you submit (after admin review and approval) to other users.
  • Process and manage your subscription through RevenueCat.
  • Send you service-related communications (e.g., recipe approval status).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google LLC) for authentication, database, storage, analytics, and App Check (which protects our backend services from unauthorized requests); RevenueCat for subscription management; Spoonacular for recipe discovery; Google Gemini API for AI-assisted ingredient parsing and consolidation; Walmart Affiliate Product API for grocery product search. These providers process data on our behalf under their respective terms.
  • Third-Party Integrations: When you connect a Kroger account, the items in your shopping list are shared with Kroger to populate your store cart. When you choose to send a shopping list to Walmart, the names of items are sent (without account information) through our secure backend to Walmart's product search API, and the matched items are passed to Walmart through a deep link that pre-loads them into your Walmart cart.
  • Community Features: Approved recipes are displayed publicly within the App with an abbreviated name (e.g., "Drew N.") to protect your privacy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Community recipes you submitted that were approved may be retained in anonymised form.

5. Data Security

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all network communications.
  • Firebase Security Rules restricting database and storage access to authorised users only.
  • Secure on-device storage (encrypted keystore) for third-party authentication tokens.
  • OAuth 2.0 with PKCE for third-party store integrations that require user sign-in (e.g., Kroger).
  • Firebase App Check (Play Integrity on Android, DeviceCheck on iOS) to verify that requests to our backend originate from a legitimate instance of the App.
  • Server-side handling of credentials for third-party APIs (e.g., the Walmart Affiliate API private key never leaves our backend).

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Meal Lineup is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection / Restriction: Object to or request restriction of certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

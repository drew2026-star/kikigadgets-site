Chore Scramble

Privacy Policy

Kiki Gadgets Ltd.  |  Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Kiki Gadgets Ltd. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Chore Scramble mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Chore Scramble collects only a randomly generated anonymous device identifier (Firebase Anonymous Authentication UID). This identifier contains no personal information and cannot be used to identify you.

We also collect basic usage metrics, including:

  • Number of completed chores
  • Badges earned within the App

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, payment information, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information is used exclusively to provide and operate the Chore Scramble service and to maintain user sessions. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

3. Data Storage and Third Parties

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), operated by Google LLC. We do not share data with any other third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Chore Scramble explicitly supports use by children under 13. Because all accounts are fully anonymous — no email address, name, or contact information is required — the App does not collect personally identifiable information from any user, regardless of age. Parents and guardians may use the App with their children without concern about personal data collection.

5. Data Security

  • Firebase Security Rules restrict access so that only members of a group can read or write that group's data.
  • All data in transit is protected by HTTPS/TLS encryption.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Removal

You can leave a group at any time using the Leave Group option within the App. Group administrators may delete entire groups, which removes all associated data.

To request deletion of your data, contact us at support@kikigadgetsltd.com with your group code. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Effective Date" above and, where appropriate, via an in-app notification. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Kiki Gadgets Ltd.
  • Email: support@kikigadgetsltd.com
  • Website: www.kikigadgetsltd.com
